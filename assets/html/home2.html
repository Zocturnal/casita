About Us

Casita is a Mexican American comfort food restaurant. This business was built from the ground up on a foundation of the bonds of chosen family and togetherness. This space we've created is one where people could come together and just enjoy each other's company with good food and good conversation. Like coming home after a long stressful day to people who bring you joy and happiness. All while enjoying good food that fills you with just as much warmth and love as the people around you.
